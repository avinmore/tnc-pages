About Rutuma

Designed for Every Woman's
Unique Cycle

Rutuma is a comprehensive period and ovulation tracking app built with privacy, accuracy, and simplicity at its core.

Every woman's body is unique. Rutuma learns from your personal cycle data to deliver accurate predictions for your period, fertile window, and ovulation — no two users get the same experience.

Unlike most period trackers, Rutuma stores all your data entirely on your device. Your intimate health information never leaves your phone. There is no cloud sync to our servers, no data mining, no sharing with advertisers.

From cycle logging and symptom tracking to medicine reminders and sound therapy for wellness — Rutuma is the all-in-one health companion built around your needs.

What Rutuma Does

Everything You Need,
Nothing You Don't

Rutuma combines smart cycle intelligence with daily wellness tools — all in one beautiful, easy-to-use app.

🌸

Period Tracking

Log your period start and end dates. Rutuma learns your cycle pattern and predicts future periods with increasing accuracy over time.

🌕

Ovulation Prediction

Know your fertile window before it arrives. Rutuma calculates ovulation using your unique cycle data and displays a visual moon-cycle wheel.

📋

Symptom Logging

Track mood, pain, energy, discharge, and 30+ custom symptoms. See patterns across your cycle at a glance in the Insights tab.

💊

Medicine Reminders

Never miss a pill or supplement. Set smart daily reminders for any medicine with custom schedules and snooze support.

🔒

Encrypted Data Backup

Back up your entire cycle history as an encrypted file and restore it anytime — on a new device or after reinstalling the app.

🎵

Sound Therapy

Calming audio sessions curated for each phase of your cycle — from energizing morning sounds to sleep-inducing night music.

Privacy First

Your Health Data
Stays With You

We built Rutuma on a simple principle: your intimate health data belongs to you and only you. Period.

📱

Device-Only Storage

All cycle data, symptoms, and logs are stored exclusively on your device. We have zero access to it.

🚫

No Data Mining

We never sell, share, or use your health data for advertising. Your body is not a data product.

🔐

Secure Backup

When you back up to Google Drive, files are encrypted and stored only in your personal Drive app folder — inaccessible to anyone else.

✅

ATT Compliant

We follow Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. Tracking only happens with your explicit consent.

Our Mission

Empowering Women Through
Health Awareness

We believe every woman deserves tools that respect their privacy and support their wellbeing.

"Health awareness should be simple, private, and accessible to every woman — regardless of where she is in the world."

At Team Rutuma, we are building a space where women feel safe to track their bodies honestly. We started Rutuma because we saw a gap: most period trackers were either too basic, or too invasive with user data. We wanted to create something that combined beautiful design, intelligent predictions, and uncompromising respect for privacy — all in a single app available globally. Our mission is to make personal health tracking effortless, joyful, and completely private for every woman.
